III. Truth

it’s then that I woke from the trance

regretful and hopeful, bathed in light

anthems singing clear and bright

a revel from loss into a chance

a chance to unite all—

a charismatic clown and impossible luddite;

an avid evangelist with a stoic skeptic;

For the fringes who once grasped hope

like clutching the reins of a breaking horse,

turn into a chorus of talent and life’s intent.

IV. Together

Alas, the hidden one is still dreaming

a vessel of stories once told

of a life deeply intervening

with context and meaning

of how to make us whole

this one is hiding in plain sight

as plain as can be, among those who unite

in all but the wanton strife

claiming a life of purpose, for all of eternity.
