About Molly

"The question of what makes a philosopher a philosopher, and what makes a poet a poet, is one I have stopped asking."

I am a writer and strategist based in NYC, dedicated to the art of precise documentation and creative expression. In a world that often feels chaotic, I look for structure—whether that is in the architecture of a digital archive or the rhythm of a stanza.

My career has been defined by navigating high-velocity environments, helping teams find clarity in noise. But my work is also deeply personal. I am driven by an interest in the human condition—why we do what we do, and how we record it.

Outside of work, life is a balance of co-parenting a teenager, learning Japanese, and slowly restoring a 19th-century schoolhouse.

Philosophy & Approach

  • Humanity First: Technology serves us, not the other way around.
  • Clarity over Volume: Saying less, but saying it better.
  • Integration: Merging the strategic with the poetic.
