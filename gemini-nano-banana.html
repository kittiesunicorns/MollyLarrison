Why This Works

The Gem explained its approach using prompt engineering best practices:

  • Clear instructions: Rather than a vague request, it broke the task into step-by-step components (character mapping, visuals, expressions, setting)
  • Entity inputs: It treated the comedians and TV characters as distinct entities, helping the model separate the subject (comedian) from the style target (character)
  • Decomposition: Instead of one long description, it separated the request into manageable piecesâ€”Visuals, Expression, and Setting
  • Contextual constraints: Adding the Miami living room detail gave the model specific parameters for a cohesive image
